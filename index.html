Discover Norzagaray

Welcome to the hidden gem of the Philippines—Norzagaray, Bulacan! Nestled in the heart of this picturesque province, Norzagaray is a haven for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking for a serene escape. With its meandering rivers, mysterious cave, and world-class resorts, Norzagaray offers an unforgettable journey into the beauty of nature and relaxation.

About Norzagaray, Bulacan

Did you know that the town of Norzagaray was originally named for a bird, but the name was later changed to Norzagaray?

Norzagaray is abundant in historical significance. Prior to this, it was merely a barrio inside the municipality of Angat. The area was once referred to the barrio "Casay". Casay was a prevalent avian species in the area during that time.
